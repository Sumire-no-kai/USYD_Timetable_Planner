USYD Timetable Planner

Capture classes, avoid clashes, and plan University of Sydney timetable preferences locally in your browser.

Privacy Policy Support Page
Independent student-made tool. This extension is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially supported by the University of Sydney. Generated plans are for planning reference only.

Local-first

Captured timetable options and planner settings are stored in the user's browser with Chrome local storage.

No tracking

The extension has no backend server, account system, analytics service, advertising service, or tracking service.

Planning only

Users should manually confirm all official preferences, times, locations, availability, and teaching weeks in Sydney Timetable.

What it does

USYD Timetable Planner reads class option tables from Sydney Timetable only after the user clicks the capture button. It can compare class times, avoid timetable clashes, show a weekly preview, and export a readable CSV plan for review.
